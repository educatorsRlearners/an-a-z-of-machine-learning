E is for Exploratory Data Analysis

What is Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), why is it done, and how do we do it in Python?

Jun 9, 2020 â€¢ 6 min read

What is Exploratory Data Analysis(EDA)?

EDA is the process of getting to know our data.

Why is it done?

Simply put, before fitting a model, we need to have a basic understanding of the type, quality, and the possible relationships between the features in our data.

How do we do it in Python?
